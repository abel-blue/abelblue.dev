    Context-Aware Chatbot with Role-Based Access


  • April 2022
  • I am the creator of a context-aware and role-based access chatbot, a cutting-edge Retrieval Augmented Generation chatbot meticulously engineered to elevate user interactions by delivering succinct and accurate responses tailored to the provided context. The chatbot adheres to a robust set of guiding principles, ensuring optimal performance and user satisfaction:
    Contextual Precision: The chatbot relies exclusively on the contextual cues at hand to craft responses, guaranteeing relevance and precision.
    Acknowledging Gaps: In cases where the context is insufficient, The chatbot transparently acknowledges its limitations, fostering trust and openness in interactions.
    Code Clarity: When presenting code examples, The chatbot employs triple backticks for proper formatting, specifying the programming language to enhance clarity and understanding.
    Focused Information: The chatbot avoids generating pseudo-code or unrelated information, maintaining consistency by delivering content that is consistently on point.
    Conciseness: Prioritizing brevity and clarity, The chatbot encapsulates responses within 600 characters or less, ensuring efficient and effective information delivery.
    Beyond these core features, The chatbot is designed with advanced capabilities:
    Role-Based Access Control: The chatbot is aware of the user's role, distinguishing between trainees and administrators. Trainees can access public data/information, such as details on 10 academy. Administrators, on the other hand, have privileged access to confidential or private information, ensuring the right level of data security.
    Conversation Chain Retention: The chatbot possesses the unique ability to retain and comprehend past conversation chains. This feature enables a seamless and coherent flow of interactions, making each conversation feel like a continuation rather than a series of disjointed queries.
    Feedback-Driven Learning: The chatbot is continuously evolving through user feedback. Each interaction serves as an opportunity for learning and adjustment, allowing the chatbot to adapt and enhance its responses over time. This iterative learning process ensures a personalized and improving conversational experience for users.
    The chatbot represents a leap forward in chatbot technology, combining contextual awareness, role-based access control, and conversation chain retention to provide users with an unparalleled and tailored conversational experience.
  • Used techstacks: Chatbot, Prompt Engineering, Python, Vector Database, LLM Prompt, AI Bot, AI Chatbot, Generative AI, Machine learning

    Data warehouse for pNEUMA | open-traffic datasets


  • June 2022
  • In June 2022, we undertook the development of a robust and scalable data warehouse solution tailored for handling open-traffic datasets within the framework of pNEUMA. The pipeline was augmented with dbt (Data Build Tool) for advanced data transformations, ensuring data quality and consistency across the warehouse.

    Overall, our approach aimed to optimize data handling, ensure data reliability, and empower data-driven decision-making within the domain of open-traffic datasets, supporting pNEUMA's mission with robust analytics capabilities.

    USGS LIDAR Data warehouse


  • Sep 2022
  • Our focus was on understanding water flow dynamics within maize farm fields, crucial for advancing research on new agricultural products. Maize yields vary significantly across fields despite uniform farming practices, necessitating a deeper understanding of spatial variability.     Speech Recognition: Amharic and Swahili Languages


  • March 2022
  • This project focuses on developing a robust speech recognition system capable of accurately converting spoken Amharic and Swahili languages into text. The goal is to facilitate seamless communication and interaction in regions where these languages are predominant, enhancing accessibility to digital tools and services. Leveraging machine learning frameworks like TensorFlow and PyTorch, the model integrates advanced signal processing techniques such as Fourier transforms, short-time Fourier transforms, and Mel-frequency cepstral coefficients (MFCCs). These methods are pivotal in capturing and analyzing speech patterns, ensuring high accuracy in transcription and improving usability across diverse linguistic contexts.
  • Used ML frameworks like TensorFlow, PyTorch and NLP
  • Used techniques like Fourier transforms, short-time Fourier transforms and Mel-frequency cepstral coefficients (MFCCs).
