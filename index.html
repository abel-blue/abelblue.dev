  • April 2022
  • I am the creator of a context-aware and role-based access chatbot, a cutting-edge Retrieval Augmented Generation chatbot meticulously engineered to elevate user interactions by delivering succinct and accurate responses tailored to the provided context. The chatbot adheres to a robust set of guiding principles, ensuring optimal performance and user satisfaction:
    Contextual Precision: The chatbot relies exclusively on the contextual cues at hand to craft responses, guaranteeing relevance and precision.
    Acknowledging Gaps: In cases where the context is insufficient, The chatbot transparently acknowledges its limitations, fostering trust and openness in interactions.
    Code Clarity: When presenting code examples, The chatbot employs triple backticks for proper formatting, specifying the programming language to enhance clarity and understanding.
    Focused Information: The chatbot avoids generating pseudo-code or unrelated information, maintaining consistency by delivering content that is consistently on point.
    Conciseness: Prioritizing brevity and clarity, The chatbot encapsulates responses within 600 characters or less, ensuring efficient and effective information delivery.
    Beyond these core features, The chatbot is designed with advanced capabilities:
    Role-Based Access Control: The chatbot is aware of the user's role, distinguishing between trainees and administrators. Trainees can access public data/information, such as details on 10 academy.     Conversation Chain Retention: The chatbot possesses the unique ability to retain and comprehend past conversation chains. This feature enables a seamless and coherent flow of interactions, making each conversation feel like a continuation rather than a series of disjointed queries.
    Feedback-Driven Learning: The chatbot is continuously evolving through user feedback. Each interaction serves as an opportunity for learning and adjustment, allowing the chatbot to adapt and enhance its responses over time. This iterative learning process ensures a personalized and improving conversational experience for users.
    The chatbot represents a leap forward in chatbot technology, combining contextual awareness, role-based access control, and conversation chain retention to provide users with an unparalleled and tailored conversational experience.

    USGS LIDAR Data warehouse


  • Sep 2022
  • We are very interested in how water flows through a maize farm field. This knowledge will help us improve our research on new agricultural products being tested on farms. How much maize a field produces is very spatially variable. Even if the same farming practices, seeds and fertilizer are applied exactly the same by machinery over a field, there can be a very large harvest at one corner and a low harvest at another corner. We would like to be able to better understand which parts of the farm are likely to produce more or less maize, so that if we try a new fertilizer on part of this farm, we have more confidence that any differences in the maize harvest are due mostly to the new fertilizer changes, and not just random effects due to other environmental factors. We are tasked to produce an easy to use, reliable and well designed python module that domain experts and data scientists can use to fetch, visualise, and transform publicly available satellite and LIDAR data. In particular, our code should interface with USGS 3DEP and fetch data using their API.
  • Used techstacks: LASzip, PDAL, CloudCompare, USGS 3DEP API and GIS software packages such as QGIS or ArcGIS.
